# Analysis-of-Staff-Performance-in-Restaurants

The current dataset shows the daily sales at a restaurant. The aim of the project is to find the following:
a.	The staff who is performing well
b.	To identify the best performing staff
c.	To understand each staff’s efficiency
d.	To identify the ways to maximize staff’s efficiency

Thus, to optimize the overall process efficiency and empower the restaurant’s workflow and revenue.
